Geo-Science

Geographers working in geo-science disciplines study phenomena relating to the Earth's physical environment.

This includes research into the spatial components of Earth's structure and land forms, investigation

into processes such as weather and climate, the study of environmental features such as water and plant

resources, and the search for understanding in regards to human-nature interactions such as wildlife management.

Geo-science-minded geographers today solve a wide range of Earth-related problems that span the local, regional, and global scale.
