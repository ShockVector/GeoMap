Humanities

Geographers in these fields are interested in the relationships between people, space, and place.

They explore things like political and legal boundaries, settlements, religion, language, history,

and culture. Through this interpretation and research they glean a clearer understanding of humans

and how they interact with space. These geographers are the anthropologists, theists, lobbyists,

and historians that are found teaching, researching, and writing all over.
