Tokyo's Busiest Rail Stations

The rail lines of Tokyo are among the most extensive ubran railroad systems in the world. Depicted on the map are the seven busiest stations in the Greater Tokyo Area.

I originally chose to map Tokyo's rail lines in Milestone 4 to make to use of the Google Map styler's ability to highlight certain map elements, such as rail lines, and hide other elements. I thought the extensive Tokyo rail system would be the best focus area for this.

For the objectives of this Deep Dive I decided to make markers for each of the eight busiest stations as listed on Wikipedia with statistics and steer view in order to give a feel of how busy the stations are. Clicking on a marker will show you the passenger statistics and street view.
